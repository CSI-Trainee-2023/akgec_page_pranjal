Electrical and Electronics Engineering


Electrical Engineering stream involve the study and applications of Electricity, Electronics and Electromagnetics
that entails and encompasses Power Engineering, Control Systems, Signal Processing, Digital Computers and
Instrumentation. The department is running undergraduate course in Electrical & Electronics Engineering since
2003 with a current intake of 120. Prior to this the course was Electrical Engineering since the inception of the
college in 1998. The department is also running Post Graduate course on Electrical Power and Energy System
since 2009 with the intake of 18.Now this PG course is renewed as Electrical & Electronics Engineering in 2016.
These areas of Electrical Engineering help student to enhance his or her capability to think critically & rationally,
employability and his or her utility for industry society and nation in large. Department is well equipped with
state of art labs to train electrical graduates to advance their theoretical knowledge and sound practices of the
profession. The students are encouraged to undertake projects in the areas of Machine Design, Power System Planning,
Operations and Control, Electronics and Microprocessor Applications and Power Electronics.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To produce excellent engineering professionals committed to serve the 
society & nature embedded with a strong moral character, value system and 
good leadership qualities having an urge for lifelong learning.

Mission

1. To empower the faculty members and encourage them to continually 
   develop to keep pace with the changing trends in technology.
2. To provide a conducive environment for effective teaching and learning 
   with focus on creating new knowledge, skills set and imbibing moral 
   values with professional ethics.
3. To promote the culture of research and development through effective  
   student engagement and regular infrastructural development.
4. To develop strong industry-academia alliance for practicing real time 
   projects and also involve industry in content delivery.
5. To adopt transparent practices for the overall progress and welfare of 
   the student, faculty members and staff.

HOD


Dr. Hemant Ahuja is the Professor and Head at the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. He
has over 20 years of rich and extensive experience in academics and Industry. He received his B. Tech with
honours in Electrical Engineering from Kurukshetra University in 2001. He did M. Tech. in Energy and
Environmental Management from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi in 2008. He has been awarded with Prof.
O.P. Gupta medal (Gold Medal) for securing the highest CGPA during his M.Tech Programme. He did his PhD
from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi in 2013. His area of expertise includes Electrical Machines, Power
Electronics and Wind energy conversion systems. He has over 25 Publications in various International Journals
and conferences. He has two patents filed and five patents published to his credit and completed two research
projects funded under TEQIP-III. Dr. Ahuja has been a member of several professional bodies including IEEE, PES,
ISTE and IEI. He has been working closely with the IEI-Ghaziabad local centre as an esteemed member of the
executive committee. Dr. Ahuja is Deeply involved in the Electrical Engineering related activities taken at the
Abdul Kalam Technical University level and the member of Board of studies for Electrical Engineering at AKTU.
